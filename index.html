DOCV (Doctor Vehicle)

Complete User Documentation

Introduction

DOCV (Doctor Vehicle) is a comprehensive vehicle assistance platform that connects drivers with nearby mechanics and towing services. The platform consists of three main applications:

User App

For drivers to request services and track assistance

Service Provider App

For mechanics to manage service requests and bookings

Tower App

For towing service providers to manage towing requests

Getting Started

Account Creation

To start using DOCV, follow these steps:

  • Download the appropriate app for your role (User, Service Provider, or Tower)
  • Click on "Sign Up" to create a new account
  • Fill in required information (name, email, password)
  • Verify your email address
  • Complete your profile setup
Note: Service providers and towers will need to undergo additional verification before their accounts are activated.

User App Features

Requesting Service

  • Open the app and tap the "+" icon
  • Select your vehicle from the list
  • Choose the type of service needed
  • View nearby service providers
  • Select a provider and confirm booking

Tracking Service Status

  • View real-time updates on your booking
  • Chat with service provider through the app
  • Track tower location on map (for towing services)
  • Receive notifications about service progress

Payments

  • Add payment methods securely
  • View service costs before confirmation
  • Process payments through the app
  • Access payment history and receipts

Service Provider Features

Managing Bookings

  • View incoming service requests
  • Accept or decline bookings
  • Update service progress
  • Manage multiple bookings

Service Management

  • Add and update service offerings
  • Set availability status
  • Track completed services
  • Manage service pricing

Tower App Features

Navigation and Tracking

  • Real-time map navigation to customer location
  • Route optimization
  • Live tracking updates for customers
  • Location-based service area management

Job Management

  • Accept towing requests
  • View customer and vehicle details
  • Track earnings and completed jobs
  • Manage availability status

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Location Services

Ensure location services are enabled on your device

Payment Issues

Verify your payment method and billing information

Connection Problems

Check your internet connection and try refreshing the app
